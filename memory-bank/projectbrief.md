# NeoDemos Project Brief

**North Star**: To build a deeply contextual, AI-powered assistant ("NeoDemos") for local government officials (such as city councillors in Rotterdam). It transforms fragmented, voluminous council documents—like meeting agendas, proposals, histories, and party programs—into easily digestible, actionable insights filtered through the ideological lens of individual political parties.

**Core Objectives**:
1. **Efficiency**: Reduce the time councillors spend parsing through thousands of pages of documents.
2. **Contextual Awareness**: Leverage Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to ensure LLM analyses are grounded in the city's historical meeting context and past decisions.
3. **Ideological Personalization ("Party Lens")**: Evaluate the alignment of current agenda proposals against the stated core values and past behavior of specific political parties (e.g., GroenLinks-PvdA).
4. **Reliable Extensibility**: Build a robust foundation starting with the Web, designed such that future iOS (iPhone/iPad) and MacOS native apps can seamlessly consume the same unified APIs and data structures.

**Current Proof-of-Concept Scope**:
Focuses on Rotterdam (2024-2026 data). Provides a complete calendar view of committee and council meetings, agenda item summaries, comprehensive AI analysis, and the "Standpuntanalyse" (Party Lens evaluation).
